Photo Editing

Photo editing refers to the process of altering or enhancing images using colorful ways and software tools to achieve asked visual results. The purpose of photo editing can range from perfecting the overall aesthetics of a print to correcting specific defects or enhancing specific rudiments. Photo editing is extensively used in colorful fields, including photography, graphic design, marketing, and social media.

Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video footage to produce a finished and polished product. Video editors use technical software to cut, trim, and arrange video clips, add visual and audio goods, acclimate colors, and enhance overall visual and audile quality. The thing of video editing is to tell a compelling and coherent story, whether it's for entertainment, educational purposes, marketing, or any other intended use.
